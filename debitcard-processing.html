Debit Card Processing

Accepting debit cards at your business has key benefits for your customers

Debit card use is increasing every day. In fact, industry analysts report that nearly one third of U.S. consumers prefer using debit cards to make everyday purchases over any other form of payment.

Add debit card processing to your merchant account, and give your customers an additional payment option. Debit card is secure and convenient for both merchants and consumers. With debit card acceptance, merchants are free from the risks and hassle of checks. In addition, debit card transactions are less expensive to process than credit card transactions, allowing you to keep more of your profits. Consumers benefit from the speed and security of the debit card transaction, plus they enjoy the added convenience of optional cash back.

We can process your debit card transactions quickly and easily through our direct connection to regional and national debit card processing networks.

Debit card transaction benefits:

  • Increase you sale
  • Reduced check out time
  • More convenient than accepting checks
  • No signature required
  • PIN and Chip access provides additional security
  • Bad checks, credit card chargebacks, and counterfeit worries are eliminated
